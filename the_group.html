The Wanda Group

The Wanda Group was founded in 1988 and is engaged in three key business areas - commercial properties, culture, and finance. In 2015, its assets amounted to USD $97.39 billion (RMB ¥634 billion) with a revenue of USD $44.57 billion (RMB ¥290.16 billion). Wanda Cultural Industry Group, is its largest cultural enterprise in China, and the world's largest cinema operator. It is also the world's biggest sports company.

About Wanda Group

  • China’s two largest entertainment industry overseas acquisitions :
    • USD $3.5 billion acquisition of Legendary Entertainment
    • USD $2.6 billion acquisition of AMC
  • China’s largest cultural enterprise : RMB ¥34.1 billion revenue in 2014
  • China’s largest scale investment for cultural tourism project : Wuhan Central Culture District
  • World’s only indoor film theme park : Wanda Wuhan Film Theme Park
  • World’s largest cinema chain operator : over 1300 cinemas
  • World’s first large scale cultural tourism complex : Wanda Cultural Tourism City
  • China’s premier commercial real estate brand : Wanda Plaza
  • China’s best corporate training center : Wanda Institute
  • Asia’s largest property owner : 21.5 million square meters

Wanda Film Holdings

The Chinese Box Office

China is the world’s most coveted film destination. Since 2010, China’s film industry has entered a “Golden Decade”; where in 2015 alone, its box office size has grown to US $6.5 billion. China is now the second largest box office in the world, and is projected to become the world’s largest by 2017.
