  • T-lots

    With a multitude of customizable options throughout the facility, temporary “T-” lots can cover the expanse of every imagination.
  • Surface of set building area is <3% gradient, crushed granite
  • 60t loading suitable for cranes and other heavy-duty vehicles
  • Shooting power supplied with 4 outdoor distribution box stations
  • 802.11ac Wi-Fi. Wireless APs (on lampposts) every 30m along the backlot perimeter, serving as Wi-Fi Root Bridge and equipped with directional high-gain antennas that focus signals
  • In the backlot pad area, temporary Wi-Fi access points are deployed and bridged to the nearest APs in the perimeter
  • All entrances are monitored with CCTV and protected with access control
