  • Paint / Spray Shops

    Title Style

    • Mezzanine office space overlooking workshop (200sqm)
    • Spray booths fitted with carbon filters will be supplied in each of the paint workshops.
    • Three phase and domestic power throughout the workshop space at a minimum of 10 x 32Amp and 10 x 16Amp cee form socket outlets. Domestic power at 10m intervals.
    • Compressed air for pneumatic tools supplied around periphery of workshop.
    • High speed network connectivity.
    • Break room with kitchenette supplied
    • Industrial sinks with hot and cold water.
    • Paint wash stations with appropriate trade waste arrestor/slurry tanks supplied

    • Storage cages or dangerous goods cabinets for paint storage
    • Hand and eye wash stations
