The
Wanda Group

The Wanda Group was founded in 1988 and is engaged in three key business areas - commercial properties, culture, and finance. In 2015, its assets amounted to USD $97.39 billion (RMB ¥634 billion) with revenue of USD $44.57 billion (RMB ¥290.16 billion). Wanda Cultural Industry Group, is its largest cultural enterprise in China, and the world's largest cinema operator. It is also the world's biggest sports company.

China is now the second largest box office in the world, and is projected to become the world’s largest by 2017.
